# Concepts

SACP is a **persistent, multi-sovereign** AI conversation: orchestrated turn loop, optional autonomous operation, human interjections, proposals, and strong **per-participant** control over provider, model, spend, and prompts.

Details stay in the repo so they stay accurate.
